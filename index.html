Aircraft Database


For this independent project, I created a sortable, filterable, and searchable database of all aircraft designs listed on wikipedia. I believe this is the first free database of that type online. To create the database, I scraped the aircraft specifications and wikipedia page content for all the aircraft listed on wikipedia, parsed that data, and created a database with it.

The final database has approximately 10,000 aircraft, and includes specifications like: wingspan, length, height, empty weight, gross weight, maximum speed, cruise speed, and stall speed. The database also includes the manufacturer, aircraft role, number of aircraft built, first flight year, and national origin.
